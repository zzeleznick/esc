Engineering Student Council

Official


Passionate about healthcare?

And mystified by the opportunities in this field? Do you want to learn more about the technological and business side of health?


Enjoy learning and networking?

Learn from key members of industry and meet with top companies such as Kaiser Permanente, Frog Design, and Salesforce.

Our Mission



Community Support

Strengthening our community to explore diverse opporunities in healthcare.

Speaker Sessions

Hosting local events to discuss the frontiers of healthtech innovation, design, and entrepreneurship.

Student Engagement

Empowering students to take active roles in spanning new directions of medicine, technology, and business.



Our Team




Ada Gu

Industry Liaison, Berkeley Edge

As a Junior at UC Berkeley majoring in Public Health and French, Ada has always been passionate about the possibilities in healthcare, particularly the intersection of healthcare, business and technology. She loves organizing events to help students to reach their career goals and discover their own passions. She first became inspired by healthcare innovation during her internship at Kaiser Permanente in her freshman year, but she's been addicted ever since!
